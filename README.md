### 🏛️ The Digital Forge
Welcome to **Masonode**. We are a multi-chain development collective focused on creating structured, high-performance infrastructure for the decentralized future. Our mission is to transform complex, multi-network data into secure, actionable intelligence.

### 🧱 Our Core Pillars
* **Protocol Interoperability:** Bridging liquidity and utility between isolated ecosystems.
* **Network Intelligence:** Real-time monitoring and analytics for cross-chain activity.
* **Modular Infrastructure:** Designing non-custodial asset vaults with universal security standards.
